Our Story

How it all began...

Founded in 2025, our mission is to find loving homes for rocks of all shapes and sizes. What started as a small collection of garden pebbles has grown into the world's premier pet rock adoption facility.

Our Mission

We believe every rock deserves a warm windowsill to call home. Our expert geologists carefully select each specimen for personality and charm.

Rock Care Tips

  • Dust your rock weekly with a soft brush
  • Keep away from paper (they hate paperwork)
  • Regular sunbathing is recommended
  • Never feed after midnight
